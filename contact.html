Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password. The link in the email will expire after 24 hours for security reasons. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder. For additional help, contact our support team with your registered email address and we'll assist you in regaining access to your account.

2. Can I submit my own recipes to BiteNestle?

Absolutely! We love featuring recipes from our community. Use our recipe submission form (available in your account dashboard or at bitenestle.com/submit-recipe) to share your original recipes. Please include detailed instructions, ingredient lists, and any personal story about the recipe. Our editorial team reviews all submissions and may contact you for additional information. While we can't publish every submission, we appreciate all contributions.

3. How do I report an error in a recipe?

We strive for accuracy in all our recipes. If you find an error, please use the "Report Error" button at the bottom of each recipe page or email corrections@bitenestle.com with the recipe title, specific error details, and your suggested correction. Our culinary team will review the report and make necessary updates. We appreciate your help in maintaining the quality of our recipes for all users.

4. Do you offer nutrition information for your recipes?

Many of our recipes include approximate nutrition information calculated from standard ingredient databases. However, these are estimates only, as actual nutritional content can vary based on specific ingredients used and portion sizes. We recommend consulting a nutritionist for precise dietary needs. You can find nutrition information (when available) at the bottom of recipe pages. We're working to add this information to more recipes over time.

5. How can I unsubscribe from emails?

You can manage your email preferences at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email from us or by adjusting your notification settings in your account dashboard. Please note that even if you unsubscribe from marketing emails, you may still receive important service-related communications about your account. If you're having trouble unsubscribing, contact our support team and we'll assist you.

6. Are your recipes suitable for people with food allergies?

While we include allergy information when possible, we cannot guarantee that any recipe is completely free of specific allergens due to variations in ingredients and preparation methods. Always check ingredient labels carefully and modify recipes as needed for your dietary requirements. We're working to add more allergy-friendly filters and tags to help you find suitable recipes. For severe allergies, consult with your healthcare provider before trying new recipes.

7. Can I use your recipes in my cookbook/blog/restaurant menu?

Our recipes are protected by copyright, but we often grant permission for limited non-commercial use with proper attribution. For personal blogs or family cookbooks, you may use up to 2 recipes with clear credit to "BiteNestle.com" and a link back to the original recipe. For commercial uses (including restaurant menus, published cookbooks, or large-scale reproduction), please contact us at permissions@bitenestle.com to discuss licensing options.

8. How do I save recipes to my account?

To save recipes, simply click the "Save to My Recipes" button (heart icon) on any recipe page. You must be logged in to your account to use this feature. Saved recipes will appear in your "My Recipe Box" section of your account dashboard. You can organize saved recipes into custom collections and add personal notes. This feature is available to all registered users at no cost.

9. Why can't I see comments on recipes anymore?

We periodically update our community features to improve user experience. If you can't see comments, it may be because: (1) comments are temporarily disabled for maintenance, (2) the recipe is new and doesn't have comments yet, or (3) your account has community restrictions due to previous violations of our guidelines. If you believe this is an error, please contact our support team with details about the recipe and your account information.

10. Do you have a mobile app?

Yes! The BiteNestle app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. The app includes all the features of our website plus exclusive mobile-only content and tools like meal planning, shopping lists, and step-by-step cooking mode. The app is free to download with optional premium features available through in-app purchase.
